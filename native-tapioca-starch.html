NATIVE TAPIOCA STARCH

Native tapioca starch is a white and fine powdery by extraction from 100% fresh tapioca roots. Native tapioca starch is widely used in both food and non-food industry. Its unique properties, superior performance and competitive price has made the local and global demand for Native tapioca starch increase significantly.
